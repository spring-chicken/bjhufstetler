About the Author

I am a mechanical engineer and space operations officer serving in the United States Space Force as a NASA CapCom for the International Space Station. I have worked as the Space Sciences Department Head at the Space Force's premier technical school, as the chief satellite engineer of a $30B constellation, as an air quality monitoring engineer, as a middle school English and Calculus teacher in South Korea, and as a fantastic line cook and dishwasher.

I love teaching orbital mechanics and exploring Fermi style rabbit holes. I also like running, biking, reading, and listening to podcasts. You can find me on X but my feed is mostly just a bunch of space news reposts.

Fun fact: My five-person D&D group played the same campaign every week for eight straight years! I am also honored to have been the proud recipient of the GeeklyInc Geekly of the Year Award.

All opinions are my own.
